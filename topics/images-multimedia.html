HTML Images and Multimedia

HTML allows embedding multimedia content like images, videos, audio, and external resources to create rich web experiences.

1. Embedding Images with <img>

The <img> tag is used to embed images in an HTML page. It requires the src attribute (source of the image) and optionally includes the alt attribute (alternative text for accessibility).

Syntax:

<img src="image-url" alt="description" />

Example:

<img src="https://via.placeholder.com/150" alt="Placeholder Image" style="border: 1px solid #ccc; padding: 5px;" />

2. Adding Videos with <video>

The <video> tag embeds video content. It supports attributes like controls, autoplay, and loop.

Syntax:

<video src="video-url" controls autoplay loop>
  Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>

Example:

<video src="https://www.w3schools.com/html/mov_bbb.mp4" controls autoplay loop width="320" height="240">
  Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>

3. Adding Audio with <audio>

The <audio> tag embeds audio content. It supports similar attributes as the <video> tag, including controls and autoplay.

Syntax:

<audio src="audio-url" controls autoplay>
  Your browser does not support the audio tag.
</audio>

Example:

<audio src="https://www.w3schools.com/html/horse.mp3" controls autoplay>
  Your browser does not support the audio tag.
</audio>

4. Embedding External Content with <iframe>

The <iframe> tag allows embedding external web pages or content into the current page.

Syntax:

<iframe src="https://www.example.com" width="600" height="400">
  Your browser does not support iframes.
</iframe>

Example:

<iframe src="https://www.wikipedia.org" width="600" height="300" style="border: 1px solid #ccc;">
  Your browser does not support iframes.
</iframe>

5. Embedding YouTube Video with <iframe>

The <iframe> tag can also be used to embed a YouTube video.

Syntax:

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/video-id" width="560" height="315" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Example:

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/dQw4w9WgXcQ" width="560" height="315" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>
